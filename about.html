About Me

Hey — I’m Kushal, a high school student passionate about medicine, sports science, and data. I created NextGen Med to break down health topics for curious people like me, from NBA injuries to rare medical conditions. Everything you see here — design, content, research — is built from scratch.

This isn’t just a blog. It’s a long-term project I’ll carry into college, evolving with new research, interviews, and community features.

Thanks for reading — and if you’re a student or professional who wants to collaborate, reach out!
